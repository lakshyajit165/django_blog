About This App

This App was built in the process of exploring Django - An awesome python web framework.

I love experimenting with new technologies and programming languages. I had been reading blogs about Django framework since sometime, but had not tried it out. I was thinking, it would be a great exprience if I could build something out of it. And then, one thing I knew, I kept exploring this framework. Youtube, Google And even StackOverflow came to my rescue. I must say, I haven't done with it yet. I wish to explore even more functionalities and intricacies related to this framework.

With this App you can -

  • Create your profile.
  • Add your Profile Pic.
  • Create, View, Update and Delete Posts.
  • Add a photo to your posts too!
  • Change your password in case you forget it!
